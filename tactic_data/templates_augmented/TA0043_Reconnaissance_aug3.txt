======================================================================
  MITRE ATT&CK — TACTIC: TA0043
======================================================================
  ID         : TA0043
  NAME       : Reconnaissance

  OBJECTIVE  : The adversary is attempting to obtain information for planning future operations.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
  TECHNIQUES USE
----------------------------------------------------------------------

  Reconnaissance consists of adversary techniques for gathering
  information through active or passive means to support targeting.
  This may include details about the organization, its infrastructure,
  or its personnel.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
  OUTCOME / TACTIC LINKS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

  This information can be leveraged in later stages of the adversary
  lifecycle, such as supporting Initial Access planning, guiding
  post-compromise prioritization, or enabling additional reconnaissance
  activities.

----------------------------------------------------------------------

  No Linux detection strategies found for this tactic.
